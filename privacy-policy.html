Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Home / Privacy Policy

Money Wise 2026 (“we,” “our,” or “us”) operates the website located at moneywise2026.com (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit our Site. Please read this policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the Site.

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting personally identifiable information you may provide us through the Site. This policy describes the types of information we may collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding our use of your information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Voluntarily

You can browse most of our Site without providing any personal information. However, you may choose to provide personal information in certain situations, such as:

  • When you submit a contact form, including your name, email address, and message contents
  • When you subscribe to our email newsletter, including your email address
  • When you correspond with us by email
  • When you respond to surveys or reader feedback requests

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you visit the Site, we and our service providers may automatically collect certain technical information from your device, including:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring website or source
  • Pages viewed, time spent, and navigation paths
  • Device identifiers and screen size
  • Date and time of access

This information is collected through cookies, web beacons, log files, and similar tracking technologies. We use this data primarily to understand how the Site is used, improve performance and content, detect abuse, and serve relevant advertising.

2. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by websites you visit. We use the following categories of cookies:

2.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the Site to function properly. They enable basic features such as page navigation and access to secure areas. The Site cannot function properly without these cookies.

2.2 Analytics Cookies

We may use analytics services, such as Google Analytics, to understand how visitors interact with our Site. These cookies collect information in an aggregated, anonymous form, including the number of visitors, pages viewed, traffic sources, and basic demographic information.

2.3 Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense and may use other third-party advertising networks to display ads on our Site. These services may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to our Site and other websites on the internet. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to our Site and other sites across the internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings. You can also opt out of some third-party vendors’ use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting aboutads.info or the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can configure your browser to refuse all cookies, accept only certain cookies, or notify you when a cookie is set. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of this and many other websites.

3. Google AdSense Disclosure

Money Wise 2026 participates in the Google AdSense program. Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to our website or other websites. Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to our users based on their visit to our Site and/or other sites on the internet.

For more information about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services, please visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

3.1 Visitor Analytics & Fingerprinting

In addition to the third-party analytics described above, Money Wise 2026 operates its own first-party visitor analytics system to monitor traffic quality, detect automated abuse, and protect against fraudulent activity. When you visit the Website, our system may automatically collect the following information for security and analytics purposes:

  • Network information: IP address, internet service provider, autonomous system number (ASN), approximate country location, and indicators of proxy, VPN, Tor, or datacenter origin.
  • Browser fingerprint: User-agent string, browser name and version, operating system, installed fonts, language preferences, time zone, screen resolution, color depth, pixel ratio, and hardware concurrency.
  • Device characteristics: Approximate device memory, touch capability, battery status (if available), and connection type.
  • Rendering signals: WebGL renderer/vendor, Canvas rendering hash, AudioContext hash — used solely to distinguish unique devices and detect emulated/automated browsers.
  • Engagement signals: Anonymous counts of mouse movement, scroll depth, clicks, keystrokes, and tab visibility changes during your session.
  • Session metadata: Referrer URL, landing page, current page, UTM parameters, and approximate session duration.

This data is used to:

  • Identify and block automated bots, scrapers, and fraudulent traffic.
  • Calculate an internal “risk score” that helps protect the Site against invalid activity.
  • Generate aggregate traffic-quality reports for internal use only.
  • Improve site performance and content based on real engagement patterns.

This data is stored on servers we control and is never sold, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. We retain individual visitor records for up to 90 days, after which records are aggregated or deleted. You may request access to or deletion of the data we hold about you at any time by emailing contact@moneywise2026.com.

If you do not consent to this analytics collection, you can prevent most of it by enabling your browser’s “Do Not Track” setting, using privacy-focused browser extensions (such as uBlock Origin, Privacy Badger), or browsing in private/incognito mode with cookies disabled. Note that disabling these features may affect site functionality.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To operate, maintain, and improve our Site and content
  • To respond to your inquiries, comments, and feedback
  • To send you newsletter emails if you have subscribed
  • To analyze usage trends and measure the effectiveness of our content
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, or abuse
  • To serve relevant advertising through third-party networks
  • To comply with applicable laws and legal requests

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personally identifiable information to third parties. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf, such as web hosting, email delivery, analytics, and advertising. These providers have access to your information only to perform these tasks and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities, such as a court order or government subpoena.

5.3 Business Transfers

If Money Wise 2026 is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred. We will notify you before your information is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

6. Third-Party Links

Our Site may contain links to third-party websites and services. We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services. We strongly advise you to review the privacy policy of every site you visit.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical security measures to protect the information we collect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our Site is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 California Residents (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. These rights include:

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, disclose, and sell
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information (note: we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your CCPA rights

To exercise these rights, please contact us at contact@moneywise2026.com.

9.2 European Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Switzerland, you have certain rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), including:

  • The right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction of inaccurate data
  • The right to request erasure (the “right to be forgotten”)
  • The right to restrict or object to processing
  • The right to data portability
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details in the “Contact Us” section below.

10. Data Retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law. Newsletter subscriber information is retained until you unsubscribe. Contact form submissions are retained for up to two years for record-keeping purposes.

11. International Data Transfers

Information we collect may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using our Site, you consent to such transfers. We take steps to ensure that any international transfer of your information is protected by appropriate safeguards.

12. Do Not Track Signals

Some browsers offer a “Do Not Track” (DNT) feature that signals websites that you do not want your online activity tracked. Because there is no universal standard for how DNT signals should be interpreted, our Site does not currently respond to DNT signals. We do, however, honor opt-out mechanisms offered by Google and other third-party advertising networks.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. When we update the policy, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through a prominent notice on the Site. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

14. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: contact@moneywise2026.com
  • Through our contact page

We will respond to all legitimate privacy inquiries within thirty (30) days. For requests under the GDPR or CCPA, we may need to verify your identity before fulfilling your request.

By using Money Wise 2026, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.
